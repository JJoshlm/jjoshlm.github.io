Server Rules

📜 1. Respect & Conduct

  • Zero Tolerance: Bullying, racism, hate speech, harassment, or toxic behavior is strictly prohibited in World, Private, or Alliance chats.
  • Community Standard: Treat all players with respect to maintain a healthy gaming environment.
  • Reporting: You must provide screenshots/proof when reporting a violation to leadership.

🛡️ 2. NAP Protection Rules

The following actions against NAP Alliances and their Academies are prohibited:

🚫 No Attacking
🚫 No Scouting

💎 3. Resource & Map Etiquette

  • Tile Safety: Attacking resource tiles is strictly forbidden. Let players farm in peace.

🚛 4. Caravans & Black Ops

Governed by a Three-Strike System:

  • Strike 1 & 2: Reported by R4s. Offender receives a formal warning.
  • Strike 3: Results in a Single Base Hit penalty.
  • Conflict Resolution: Victims may waive the strike report if an apology is accepted.

🤝 5. Member Poaching

  • Active Recruiting: Messaging members of other NAP alliances to switch is prohibited.
  • Player Autonomy: Players are free to leave and join alliances voluntarily.
  • Applications: "Walk-in" applicants are allowed, provided no prior solicitation occurred.

📉 6. Other Alliances

Fair Play: Attacking smaller alliances because they are outside the NAP is forbidden.

🕊️ 7. Diplomacy & Conflict Resolution

  1. Private Resolution: Handle disputes privately between Alliance Leads/Diplomats first.
  2. Escalation: If unresolved, bring to NAP Leadership.
⚠️ Do not bring rule disputes, grievances, or drama into General or World Chat. Keep it to private channels.

🎓 8. Academies

  • Designation: Each NAP alliance may protect one academy.
  • Governance: Academies entering the Top 10 do not receive voting rights while they maintain academy status.

⚠️ 9. General Rule Violations

(Except #4)

  • 1st Offense: Official Warning.
  • 2nd Offense: Removal from alliance or NAP Blacklist.
  • Blacklist Policy: Prohibits joining any NAP-protected alliance.

Capitol Rotation
